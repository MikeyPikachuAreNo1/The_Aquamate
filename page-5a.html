Sign Language in Swimming pool

Sign Language can be important for deaf swimmers because...

-Deaf swimmers may not hear instructions: Deaf swimmers may not be able to hear what the coaches is saying, which can lead to misunderstandings and mistakes.

-Sign language can help swimmers feel included: Sign language can help swimmers feel included in swimming lessons and enjoy them as much as their hearing peers.

-Sign language can help coaches communicate: Coaches can learn sign language to communicate with deaf swimmers.

-Sign language can help coaches be more aware: Coaches can learn to be more deaf aware by using basic sign language.
