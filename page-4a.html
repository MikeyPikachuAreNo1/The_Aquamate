The Aqua Mate

The Aqua Mate

I design a kit using Microbit to coach deaf communities for synchronized swimming
lessons in a more effective way. My project has the Aqua Mate logo. An ear is blended
into a wave to symbolize deaf persons can connect to the world in the water just like everyone
The video below will demonstrate how my product works.









What is the best part of my project? - The Aqua Mate


    User friendly for coaches: All coaches who are not familiar with sign language can use this kit easily.
    Easy to follow: All swimmers just need to see the numbers and colors from the kit, no special skills needed.
    Low cost: Micro:bit is not an expensive kit like robots and machines.
    Real-time gadget: Coaches listen to the music and give instructions using the kit, and the swimmers receive the instructions immediately and act accordingly.
    Easily customizable: The numbers, colors, and speed of the kit can be changed by coding to meet different customization needs.
